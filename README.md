# Ecopow: Next-Gen Waste-to-Energy Solution

## Overview
Ecopow is a cutting-edge waste-to-energy platform that transforms biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste into **high-efficiency biofuels** through **anaerobic methanogenesis** and **advanced gasification**. By leveraging **biochemical and thermochemical conversion pathways**, Ecopow maximizes energy yield while minimizing environmental impact.

## Key Features
- **Anaerobic Digestion**: Optimized microbial consortia for enhanced **biogas** production.
- **High-Temperature Gasification**: Thermochemical conversion of non-biodegradable waste into **syngas**.
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Continuous monitoring of gas composition and process efficiency.
- **Automated Feedstock Processing**: Smart sorting and preprocessing to ensure optimal decomposition and combustion.
- **Ultra-Low Emission Design**: Advanced filtration and catalytic cracking to reduce pollutants.

## How It Works
1. **Biodegradable Waste Processing**:
   - **Microbial hydrolysis** breaks down complex organic matter.
   - **Acidogenesis & Acetogenesis** convert intermediates into volatile fatty acids.
   - **Methanogenesis** generates high-calorific biogas.

2. **Non-Biodegradable Waste Processing**:
   - **Gasification at 800-1000Â°C** produces syngas (CO, H2, CH4).
   - **Syngas purification** removes contaminants for clean combustion.

3. **Energy Utilization**:
   - **Biogas** powers turbines or fuel cells.
   - **Syngas** is converted into electricity or synthetic fuels.
